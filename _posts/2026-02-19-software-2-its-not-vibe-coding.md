How is AI-assisted development fundamentally shifting the way we verify software?

In this talk, Aaron explores how the rise of AI-powered development tools is changing the game for software engineers. This isn't "vibe coding" — it's a real shift in how we think about building and verifying software systems. Key topics include:

1. **Prompt engineering effectiveness**: how to get the most out of AI-assisted development tools.
2. **Designing feedback mechanisms**: building systems that catch AI errors before they reach production.
3. **Blast radius planning**: managing risk when your development process involves probabilistic outputs rather than deterministic ones.

Modern development increasingly involves building systems whose behavior is probabilistic rather than deterministic — and that requires new approaches to verification, testing, and risk management.
